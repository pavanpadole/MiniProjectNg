How to win a poetry contest


  1. Check the hosts other recent contests - look at the kind of poems that won

  2. Enter more contests! Most people with many trophies enter at least 10 contests at a time

  3. Critically review your work: read it out loud line by line

  4. Modern published poems use "picture-able object" metaphors to paint a scene, are more specific than abstract, and avoid over-used (cliche) phrases like 'soul', 'pain', 'head over heels'
